## **Core Idea:** 

## **"Sheba Retention AI" \- Churn Prevention & Loyalty Engine**

### **The Problem**

Sheba.xyz loses **65%** of customers after first booking. Getting customers is costing between **Tk 800-1200 per customer**, yet they vanish without a trace with a lifetime value of Tk 50,000 and more. The response rates of **generic retention** campaigns are 8-12%.

**Bottom line**: Sheba is spending a lot on **customer acquisition** but lacks a **customer retention system.**

### **Why It Matters**

* Customer retention is **5-7 times** cheaper than customer acquisition.  
* Retention growth of **5 percent \= 25-95 percen**t profit growth (Harvard Business Review).  
* Existing **35% retention \=Tk 52 cror**e loss of revenue every year.  
* Increasing competition (PathaoHome, Shohoz, Obhai).

### **Our Solution**

**Sheba Retention AI-** an Artificial intelligence (AI) engine that anticipates the customers who will leave 45-60 days before the end date and automatically initiates personalized interventions..

* **Churn prediction model:**  "Not booked in 45 days, competitor search in the past day, 78% churn risk"  
* **Personalized intervention:**  
  * High value **customer drifting**: "Tk 500 discount on all 3 bookings ahead"  
  * **Price-sensitive**: "We now have a budget service category.  
  * Complainers of quality: "Use our new verified premium providers.  
* **Re-engagement triggers:**  
  * Your AC requires seasonal service \- reserve.  
  * Karim, your **preferred electrician** is free this weekend.  
* **Loyalty tier system:** **AI** automatically **divides** the users (Bronze/Silver/Gold) into personalized benefits.  
* **Lifetime value predictor:** "This customer is worth Tk 50K over 3 years \- invest Tk 2K to retain"

**Example**:

**Alert:**

**Rahima \- Last booking 52 days ago**

**Churn risk:** 78%   **Predicted value:** Tk 45,000

**Reason:** Price-sensitive, searched competitors

**Action:** Send Tk 300 discount \+ maintenance reminder

**Expected retention:** 68%      **ROI:** 1,500%

### **What Makes It Unique**

1. **Proactive:** Before churn (68 successes out of 100\) vs winback (15 successes out of 100\) reactive.  
2. **Individual:** Various discounts on high-value, price-sensitive, and quality-focused users.  
3. **Local:** Bengali assistance, bKash/bank assimilation, knows when AC season, Eid patterns.  
4. **Explainable:** Demystifies the reasons behind customer turnover and the reasons behind certain behaviors.  
5. **ROI-oriented:** Lifetime value calculates the best investment per customer.
